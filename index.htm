I'm not just talking about my own stuff, there are some very good jams in the ninjam environment. But these are forgotten over time. If I can somehow keep a diary and categorize it, I hope it will at least be findable. There is now an advanced search section where you can do multiple keywords and list jams by genre, sound, mood and various tags.

That's why I made such a blog.

I always say that there is a need for a community for improvised music. Although it is not possible to do this with the blogspot infrastructure, it is still possible to publish your writings here as a blogger writer.

There is also an anonymous comment/chat feature for each post.
